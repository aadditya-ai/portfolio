My career journey reflects a natural progression from network infrastructure security to platform-level trust and safety operations. The shift represents an evolution from infrastructure protection to user protection, combining technical security skills with policy enforcement and customer education.

Throughout this journey, I've maintained a focus on proactive threat detection, compliance management, and cross-functional collaboration—skills that remain essential whether securing network infrastructure or protecting platform integrity.
